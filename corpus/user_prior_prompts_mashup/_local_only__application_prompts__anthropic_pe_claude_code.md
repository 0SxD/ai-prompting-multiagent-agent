## Candidate positioning

Ten years of certified teaching trained the skill of moving another person's mental model from "does not see the problem" to "sees the problem and can work it," which is the same core skill involved in translating user feedback into coherent behavioral specifications for agents.

The last two years have concentrated on AI and prompt engineering, including six-plus months as a contractor with Mercor and Micro1 leading prompt-engineering teams and building out world-simulation environments for training. In parallel, the 143_protocol_a and Trinity Dialectic artifacts generalize that contracting work into a reusable protocol stack for multi-agent systems.

The PE Claude Code role is the first position that requires the full combination: production prompt engineering, behavioral evaluation design, agent scaffold intuition, cross-organizational communication, and the patience to write protocol and reasoning documents that other operators will use. The earlier tracks are not a detour. They are the prerequisite.

## Prompt engineering rigor

The 143_protocol_a framework demonstrates prompt engineering as a system design discipline, not a prompt-tuning craft. Each behavioral rule is modular, versioned, and testable. The Zero-Assumption Mandate and 100% Confidence Gate together encode a failure mode taxonomy: what happens when agents assume, when they execute prematurely, and when they carry context across sessions without verification. The output of the engineering process is not a better prompt but a reusable protocol with eight independently auditable modules, each under 200 words, each closing with a structural canary to detect hand-off drift between sessions and between agents.

---

## Evaluation design

The Trinity Dialectic is an evaluation framework, not a quality rubric. The distinction matters: a rubric scores after the fact; a dialectic gate halts execution until all three gates clear. Logos enforces logical consistency and source citation. Pathos verifies alignment with purpose and unlocks creative inference. Ethos arbitrates the two and confirms practical executability. The 3x3 Trinity of Trinities extends this to recursive evaluation, with a hard 15-token quality floor.

---

## Safety thinking

The A2A Circuit Breaker (MI9 Gate) documents a containment design for irreversible actions in multi-agent economic systems. The design principle is that halting on uncertainty is safer than proceeding on partial confidence, even when halting is operationally expensive. The Quarantine Protocol in 143_protocol_a holds unverified research at a barrier until Trinity quorum passes, applying the same containment logic to knowledge rather than actions.

## Communication clarity

Each module in the 143_protocol_a corpus targets 200 words or fewer where feasible. The modular breakup of 143_protocol_a into eight standalone files allows a reader to start at any layer of the protocol without reading the whole system. The Trinity Dialectic overview is written as a standalone essay for a reader with no prior context of the 0sXai project.
